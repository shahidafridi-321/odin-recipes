Lasagna

Instructions For Making Lasagna

Let's do this...
Alright! we have some meal , cut the meal in small pieces . Some tomatos his to be cut in small little parts. Don't forget to cut unions as well.

take some oil in a backer and heat it.then put the everything in it to be ready for eating after a quick cook...

Ingredients

  • 1.5Kg Meal
  • 1KG Tomatos
  • 0.5KG Union
  • Cooking Oil
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Masalas
  • Mrach

Directions

  1. Make small parts of each item
  2. Put the oil in a backer
  3. Heatup the backer
  4. After the oil becomes hot put the unions,leave it for few minutes
  5. Put in the meal
  6. After 20 minutes,put the tomatos
  7. Put the salts,Masalas and Mrach...
  8. Put some water and leave it for 20 more minutes on fire...
